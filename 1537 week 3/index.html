Our Story

Discover Our Heritage

The Story Café started with an idea that “there is a story in everything.” We love coffee, music, food, and anything that can create or bring back a story such as an antique, vintage camera or a memorable music record. So in 2018, we began to think of an idea which would combine all the elements of things we loves into one restaurant.

We transformed a 30-year-old warehouse into a new concept where our dream became a reality. After 2 years of hard work, the warehouse was transformed into a full Richmond restaurant with a historical vibe that also showcased our love for the arts and opened in March 2020.
